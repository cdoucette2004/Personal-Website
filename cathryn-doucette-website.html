About Me

I’m a Family Development major at the University of Maine with a strong interest in family relationships, communication, and research. I’ve had the privilege of working in a parenting relationships research lab, where I’ve helped analyze data and review academic literature for real-world studies. This experience deepened my understanding of how parenting styles and communication patterns affect development.

Outside the lab, I’ve been a committed member of the UMaine Women’s Rugby Team. Being part of this team has taught me discipline, leadership, and the power of community. Whether it’s on the field or in a research setting, I bring the same level of dedication and teamwork.
